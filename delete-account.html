Delete Your Mummy Di Kitchen Account

At Mummy Di Kitchen, we respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to request deletion of your account and personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email with the subject "Delete My Account" to:

support@mummydikitchen.in

Include the following information:

  • Your registered mobile number
  • Your registered email address (if applicable)
  • Your full name

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account
  • Your profile information
  • Your saved delivery addresses
  • Your order history (where legally permitted)
  • Other personal information associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some transaction records and invoices may be retained as required by applicable tax, accounting, or legal regulations.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 7 business days.
